Phra Nang Cave Beach, Thailand:

Hidden gem in limestone cliffs, accessible by boat or short trek, famous cave with offerings, fine white sand, emerald waters, tranquil retreat.

Tropical paradise on Oahu, legendary surf, palm-fringed shoreline, surrounded by resorts and nightlife, near Diamond Head crater.

Archipelago of coral islands in the Indian Ocean, overwater bungalows, crystal-clear waters, pristine white sandy beaches, vibrant coral reefs.
